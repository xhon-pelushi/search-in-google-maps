# Privacy Policy for the "Search in Google Maps" Chrome Extension

_Last updated: March 3, 2026_

## Overview

The **Search in Google Maps** Chrome extension allows you to right‑click selected text on a webpage and open a Google Maps search for that text in a new tab.

This extension is designed with privacy in mind. It does **not** collect, store, or sell any personal information about you.

## Data the extension accesses

When you use the extension:

- The extension reads the **text you have currently selected** on the active page when you choose the context menu item **"Search in Google Maps"**.
- That selected text is inserted into a Google Maps search URL (e.g., `https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=...`).
- The extension then opens that URL in a **new browser tab**.

The extension does **not**:

- Track which websites you visit
- Monitor your browsing history
- Record your clicks, keystrokes, or other user activity
- Persist any logs or data to external servers or local storage

All processing happens locally in your browser, and the only network request is the standard request your browser makes to Google Maps for the search URL you chose to open.

## Data collection, sharing, and sale

The extension:

- **Does not collect** personal information or any other user data
- **Does not transmit** any data to servers controlled by the developer or any third party
- **Does not share or sell** data to advertisers or data brokers

Any information you see on Google Maps (including search results) is handled solely by Google, under Google's own terms and privacy policy.

## Permissions explained

The extension requests the following Chrome permission:

- **contextMenus** – used only to add a single right‑click menu item ("Search in Google Maps") when you have text selected. Choosing it opens a new tab to Google Maps with your selected text as the search query. No other permissions are required.

## Third‑party services

The extension opens Google Maps in a new tab. Your use of Google Maps is governed by Google's own terms of service and privacy policy. The developer of this extension does not control how Google processes data on `google.com` domains.

## Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes in a way that affects how the extension handles data, the policy will be updated and the new version will be made available at this same URL.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the extension, you can open an issue on the project's GitHub repository or contact the developer through the contact information provided there.
